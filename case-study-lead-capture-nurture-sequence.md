# Lead Capture + Nurture Sequence
### Automated Lead Response and 7-Day Follow-Up — Powered by AI

**Demo:** [Watch Loom Demo](https://www.loom.com/share/619addbded0f464b82ef0b88f9ee8458)

---

## The Problem

Most businesses lose leads not because they don't respond — but because they respond too late, too generically, or not at all after the first email. A potential client fills out a contact form, gets a templated reply, and never hears from the business again. No follow-up. No relationship. No sale.

---

## The Solution

The moment a new lead submits a form, the workflow captures their name, inquiry, and contact details, then uses an AI language model (such as Claude, GPT-4, or Gemini) to write a personalized first-touch email based on exactly what they asked about — not a generic template. The lead is simultaneously logged into a CRM tracking sheet.

Over the next 7 days, the system sends three additional follow-up emails — each with a different purpose: Day 1 adds genuine value related to their inquiry, Day 3 builds trust through a relevant example, and Day 7 delivers a soft close with a clear call to action. Every email is written by AI in a warm, conversational tone — not a mass-mail blast. If the lead replies at any point, the sequence stops automatically.

---

## The Outcome

- Every new lead gets a thoughtful, personalized response within seconds of submitting the form
- A structured 7-day follow-up sequence runs without any manual effort
- No lead falls through the cracks. No follow-up gets forgotten.
- Reply detection stops the sequence automatically — no awkward emails sent after a lead already responded
- Every lead logged to a CRM sheet with full history
