## The Problem

When a new client signs up, they're already forming an opinion. A slow response, a missing invoice, or a vague "we'll be in touch" email signals disorganization — before the work even begins.

For most service-based businesses, that first response is manual. The owner stops what they're doing, drafts a welcome email, sends an invoice, follows up with onboarding details. One by one. Every single time. Miss the window, and the client's first impression is silence.

---

## The Solution

Every new client gets a professional welcome, invoice, and onboarding email the moment they sign up — from form submission to fully onboarded, automatically. Zero effort from you.

The moment a new client submits your new client form, three professional emails land in their inbox — automatically, within seconds. No drafting, no delays, no dropped balls.

An AI language model (such as Claude, GPT-4, or Gemini) generates each email personalized to the client's name, service, and details. The automation handles the sending. You handle the work.

Three emails, sent automatically:

1. **Welcome Email** — warm and personalized, sets the tone from the first second
2. **Invoice Email** — clear amount due with a direct payment link, delivered before the client even closes their browser
3. **Onboarding Email** — exactly what to expect, when to expect it, and how to get started — so the client feels guided, not left waiting

---

## How It Works

1. New client fills out the new client form (basic details such as name, service type, payment information, etc.)
2. Details are logged automatically to a connected spreadsheet
3. All three emails are generated and sent within seconds — personalized, professional, ready to go

That's it. The business owner doesn't write a single word.
