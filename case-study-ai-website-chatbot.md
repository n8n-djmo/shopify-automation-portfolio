# AI Website Chatbot
### Instant 24/7 Customer Support — Automated

## The Problem

Most small businesses lose potential customers after hours. A visitor lands on the website at 9pm with a question — and finds nothing but a contact form that might get a reply in 24 hours. By the time someone responds, that lead has already moved on to a competitor.

Owners also spend significant time every week answering the same 10–15 questions repeatedly — about pricing, availability, services, and scheduling. Questions that never change, but still require a human to type out a fresh reply every single time.

---

## The Solution

An AI-powered chatbot that connects directly to a business's knowledge base (a simple spreadsheet containing their FAQs) and responds to customer questions instantly — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

The system is powered by an AI language model (such as Claude, GPT-4, or Gemini), which means it understands natural language rather than relying on exact keyword matches. Customers can ask follow-up questions mid-conversation, and the chatbot remembers the full exchange — so "how much does that cost?" after asking about a specific service returns the right answer, not a generic response.

If a question falls outside the knowledge base, the chatbot politely lets the customer know it will connect them with a team member. No hallucinated answers. No wrong information sent to a real prospect.

Updating the knowledge base takes seconds — the business owner just edits a row in a spreadsheet. No code changes, no developer needed.

---

## The Outcome

- Customer questions answered in seconds, not hours — including nights, weekends, and holidays
- Owner reclaims hours every week previously spent answering the same questions manually
- No leads lost to slow response times after business hours
- Knowledge base stays current with zero technical effort
- Fully reusable across any business type — the same workflow powers a plumbing company, a real estate agency, a clinic, or a coaching practice; only the knowledge base changes
