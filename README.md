## Workflows

### 1. New Order → Google Sheets Logger

Automatically logs every new order to a Google Sheet in real time.

- **Client type:** Any business that processes orders and wants automatic records without manual data entry
- **Price:** $300–500 setup
- **Demo:** [Watch Loom demo](https://www.loom.com/share/cf1fb2908f544196a25b71ffc43fe17a)

### 2. Low Stock Alert System

Checks inventory on a schedule. Sends an email alert when any product drops below a set threshold.

- **Client type:** Any business selling physical products that needs proactive inventory alerts
- **Price:** $300–500 setup

### 3. Abandoned Cart Recovery Email

Sends a personalized recovery email 1 hour after a customer abandons their cart, listing the exact items left behind.

- **Client type:** Any business with an online store experiencing cart abandonment
- **Price:** $400–700 setup

### 4. Post-Purchase Review Request

Sends a personalized review request after order fulfillment, mentioning the exact products purchased.

- **Client type:** Any business that wants to build social proof and collect reviews automatically
- **Price:** $300–500 setup

### 5. AI Email Triage System

Monitors an email inbox and automatically classifies incoming messages by category (COMPLAINT, RETURN, QUESTION, COMPLIMENT) using Claude AI, drafts a context-aware reply, and logs everything to a tracking sheet.

- **Client type:** Any business receiving high volumes of customer emails who wants AI-assisted triage and drafted responses ready for human review
- **Price:** $500–900 setup
- **Demo:** [Watch Loom Demo](https://www.loom.com/share/21f8a2f68f964e3bb9e9fe0feb0c161e)

### 6. AI Thank You Email

Automatically generates a warm, personalized thank-you email using Claude AI after every new order and sends it via email.

- **Client type:** Any business wanting personalized post-purchase communication without manual effort
- **Price:** $300–600 setup
- **Demo:** [Watch Loom Demo](https://www.loom.com/share/711107e700db4c13ba48cea61d6c41d0)

### 7. AI Product Description Generator

Pulls a product's title, type, and tags, then uses Claude AI to generate a 150-word SEO-optimized description and writes it directly back to the product page.

- **Client type:** Any business with a large or frequently updated product catalog that wants professional descriptions without hiring a copywriter
- **Price:** $400–800 setup

### 8. AI Customer Support Triage Bot

Receives incoming customer messages in real time via webhook — from a website chat widget, contact form, or live chat integration — and instantly classifies category (COMPLAINT, RETURN, QUESTION, COMPLIMENT) and urgency (HIGH, MEDIUM, LOW) using Claude AI. Drafts a reply and logs the full ticket to a tracking sheet. Response is delivered back to the requester within seconds, 24/7.

Unlike the AI Email Triage System (which monitors an inbox asynchronously for human review), this bot is designed for live, real-time intake — every message gets an instant AI-generated response the moment it arrives.

- **Client type:** Any business that wants instant automated first-response on incoming support messages — without a support team monitoring a queue
- **Price:** $500–900 setup

### 9. Restock Intelligence Report

Every Monday at 8am, pulls full inventory data and sends the business owner a plain-English restock report structured as CRITICAL / LOW / HEALTHY with action recommendations — generated by Claude AI.

- **Client type:** Any business selling physical products that wants a weekly AI-written inventory intelligence briefing delivered automatically
- **Price:** $500–900 setup + $100/mo retainer
