## Workflows

### 1. New Order → Google Sheets Logger
Automatically logs every Shopify order to a Google Sheet in real time.
- **Client type:** Any Shopify store wanting order visibility
- **Price:** $300–500 setup
- **Demo:** [Watch Loom demo](https://www.loom.com/share/cf1fb2908f544196a25b71ffc43fe17a)

### 2. Low Stock Alert System
Checks inventory every 6 hours. Sends email alert when any product drops below 5 units.
- **Client type:** Shopify stores selling physical products
- **Price:** $300–500 setup

### 3. Abandoned Cart Recovery Email
Sends a personalized recovery email 1 hour after a customer abandons their cart, listing exact items left behind.
- **Client type:** Any DTC Shopify store
- **Price:** $400–700 setup

### 4. Post-Purchase Review Request
Sends a personalized review request 7 days after order fulfillment, mentioning the exact products purchased.
- **Client type:** All Shopify stores
- **Price:** $300–500 setup

### 5. AI Email Triage System
Automatically classifies incoming customer emails (COMPLAINT, RETURN, QUESTION, COMPLIMENT) using Claude AI, drafts a context-aware reply, and logs everything to Google Sheets.
- **Client type:** E-commerce stores with high customer support volume
- **Price:** $500–900 setup
- **Demo:** [Watch Loom Demo](https://loom.com/share/21f8a2f68f964e3bb9e9fe0feb0c161e)

### 6. AI Thank You Email
Automatically generates a warm, branded thank-you email using Claude AI after every Shopify order and sends it via Gmail.
- **Client type:** Any Shopify brand wanting personalized post-purchase communication
- **Price:** $300–600 setup
- **Demo:** [Watch Loom Demo](https://www.loom.com/share/711107e700db4c13ba48cea61d6c41d0)

### 7. AI Product Description Generator
Pulls a Shopify product's title, type, and tags, then uses Claude AI to generate a 150-word SEO-optimized description and writes it directly back to the Shopify product page.
- **Client type:** Shopify stores with large or frequently updated product catalogs
- **Price:** $400–800 setup

### 8. AI Customer Support Triage Bot
Receives incoming customer messages via webhook, classifies category (COMPLAINT, RETURN, QUESTION, COMPLIMENT) and urgency (HIGH, MEDIUM, LOW) using Claude AI, drafts a reply, and logs everything to Google Sheets.
- **Client type:** E-commerce stores with high customer support volume
- **Price:** $500–900 setup

### 9. Restock Intelligence Report
Every Monday at 8am, pulls all Shopify inventory and sends the store owner a plain-English restock report structured as CRITICAL / LOW / HEALTHY with action recommendations — generated by Claude AI.
- **Client type:** Shopify stores selling physical products
- **Price:** $500–900 setup + $100/mo retainer
