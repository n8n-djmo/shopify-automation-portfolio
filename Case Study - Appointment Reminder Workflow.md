## The Problem

Appointment-based businesses lose revenue every week to no-shows. A client forgets, gets busy, or simply loses track of the booking — and the slot goes unfilled. Sending reminders manually is tedious and easy to forget, especially when managing a full calendar.

---

## The Solution

An automated appointment reminder workflow that triggers the moment a booking is made. The system sends two personalized reminder emails — one 24 hours before and one 1 hour before the appointment — with no manual work required.

When someone books through any calendar booking app (such as Cal.com, Calendly, or Acuity), the booking data is instantly captured and processed automatically. The workflow extracts the attendee's name, email, appointment type, and scheduled time, then calculates exactly when each reminder should be sent. At the right moment, an AI language model (such as Claude, GPT-4, or Gemini) writes a warm, personalized reminder email for each touchpoint and delivers it automatically.

Works with the booking tool you already use — no switching, no migration, no change to how you manage your calendar.

---

## What Was Built

- Calendar app webhook integration — triggers instantly on every new booking (works with Cal.com, Calendly, Acuity, and more)
- Automated timing logic — calculates 24hr and 1hr reminder windows from the appointment time
- AI-powered email personalization — generates natural, friendly reminder copy tailored to the attendee's name, appointment type, and timezone
- Two-email reminder sequence — delivered at precisely the right times with no manual sending

## The Result

- Both reminder emails delivered automatically with correct personalization
- Zero manual effort after initial setup
- Works for any appointment type and any timezone
- No-show risk reduced without any change to how clients book
