# Portfolio Project #1: Shopify AI Email Engine

## The Problem
Most Shopify store owners are too busy running their business to follow up with customers manually. Abandoned carts go unrecovered. New customers never get a proper thank-you. Happy buyers forget to leave reviews. The result: lost revenue, low retention, and no social proof — all from tasks that could run automatically.

## The Solution
I built a 3-workflow AI email automation engine that runs on autopilot the moment a customer interacts with your Shopify store. Every new order triggers a warm, Claude AI-written thank-you email personalized to what the customer bought. Abandoned carts automatically receive a recovery email 1 hour later. Fulfilled orders trigger a review request 7 days after delivery. No manual work. No missed follow-ups.

## The Workflows
- **Workflow 1: AI Thank-You Email** — Shopify new order → Claude writes personalized email → Gmail sends it instantly
- **Workflow 2: Abandoned Cart Recovery** — Checkout created → Wait 1 hour → Gmail sends recovery email with cart items
- **Workflow 3: Post-Purchase Review Request** — Order fulfilled → Wait 7 days → Gmail sends review request with purchased items listed

## The Results
- Every customer gets a personal thank-you within minutes of ordering
- Abandoned carts are automatically followed up — no revenue left on the table
- Review requests go out at exactly the right time, increasing the chance a happy customer actually leaves one
- Store owner saves 3–5 hours per week on manual follow-ups
